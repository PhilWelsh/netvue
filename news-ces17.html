Congratulations to NetView’s successful performance on CES 2017

CES (International Consumer Electronics Show) is the world’s largest and the most influential consumer electronics technology annual exhibition, and also the largest consumer technology industry event which was founded by CEA (Consumer Electronics Association). 2017 CES attracted more than 3800 exhibitors around the world, including 1300 exhibitors from China, there are more than 600 exhibitors from Shenzhen. Chinese enterprises play an increasingly role in the global consumer electronics, such as Huawei, Lenovo, Haier, Hisense, Changhong, Skyworth, ZTE, Mi and so on. NetView Technology is the representative enterprise in smart home products.

NetView Technology brought a variety of products including IP cameras, bulb cameras, panoramic cameras and video doorbells to CES 2017 this time, which attracted many foreign clients to the booth.

The design of booth is concise, informative and creative. It gave people a deep impression. After the four days’ successful show, we are ready for the upcoming challenges.

Now let’s have a glance at some highlight moments of CES 2017:

VueBell caught people’s eyes VueBell is a Wi-Fi video doorbell of NetView company. It is consist of two versions, one is VueBell HD (without battery), the other is VueBell Pro. Many people stopped by to check it’s well designed ID and interface.
