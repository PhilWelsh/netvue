FAQ

My netvue doorbell doesn’t work with my existing indoor chime

Your old indoor chime needs to be short circuited. The Vuebell voltage is 8-24V AC, 9-36V DC.

I Can't connect or add my device/scan QR code/reset button doesn’t work

.Please wait for 30 seconds after connecting to power, then press reset button, the blue light will flash rapidly
a. If blue light does not flash, please short circuit your existing doorbell. VueBell's voltage is 8-24V AC, 9-36DC.
b. If blue light flashes,adjust the angle and distance when you scan your QR code and make sure there is no strong light around the doorbell and your phone.
.If the scan is successful you will hear a chime

My Netvue device is having connection problems/will not connect

Please make sure the wifi is 2.4G.
Step 1. Please wait for 30 seconds after connecting to power, then press reset button, the blue light will flash. Then scan the QR code, there will be a DingDong sound if successful
Step 2. Then wait the device connect to router. Connect success, it will play a cheerful music; failed, it will play a depress music, then you need check your 2.4G WiFi password right or not.
Step 3. After hearing cheerful music, wait for the router connect to server. Please make sure the router is work then device can be added.

My Netvue device can not recieve ring notifications

Please make sure you are using the latest updated version of our APP.
Please make sure your phone settings allow Vuebell push notifications.
Please check device settings, make sure notification is on.
If still not working please log out restart your phone and Sign in again.

I Can not receive motion detection. Don't know how to set motion detection

Please make sure the wifi is 2.4G.
Step 1. Open the app
Step 2. Go to the product live stream
Step 3. Please check the top right corner Click on the settings icon
Step 4. Go to Motion detection
Step 5. Please turn on the MOTION DETECTION. Adjust the time from 0:00 Hrs to 24:Hrs (or to the hours which you would like motion detection active), Motion sensitive and motion zones also can be customized.

My Netvue device is having connection problems/will not connect

Please make sure you are using the latest updated version of our APP.
Please make sure you are using 2.4G wifi with strong signal and your device is close enough to recieve it.
If the wifi is fine, the problem may be an unstable voltage. The Vuebell voltage is 8-24V AC, 9-36V DC.
Please turn off your router and device and turn them on again.

How do i share my account?

Step 1. Open the app
Step 2. Tab into the live stream of your product
Step 3. click shared users at the bottom right corner
Step 4. press the + icon
Step 5. enter shared user’s account name.
The same account can be logged in on multiple different phones at the same time.

How do I delete pictures/Videos?

Step 1. Open the app
Step 2. Tab into the live stream of your product
Step 3. click “history” in the bottom left corner
Step 4. press and hold on an item for 2~3 seconds
Step 5. A confirmation window will open, select 'confirm'

The Time/time zone setting isn't working

Sorry, our device can't currently support daylight savings times presently. But you can adjust the time manually for example if you are in UTC -6 you can choose UTC -5 during this time which will be an hour ahead.

I am not getting notifications from my netvue doorbell when there is motion detected

if there are multiple movements in front of doorbell constantly. Our app will take pictures every 5 seconds and send you motion notifications every 5 minutes, to avoid bothering the user.

I am not getting notifications from my netvue doorbell when there is motion detected

Use flashlight or strong light to shine the Vuebell for several seconds, then turn off the light. To see if it will come back daytime mode.

The picture from my device is black & white or pink during daylight

Some of our devices record in black and white during night, if it is still black and white during the day, the sensor needs to be shown a bright light
Hold a strong light into the doorbell for a few seconds and it should fix the problem.

The indoor chime is not making any noise.

Remove the indoor chime battery and put it inside again, then press the doorbell button. The indoor chime should work again.

The recorded motion clip images are not showing, but show a grey stock image

This means there has been a problem downloading or loading the clips
Step 1. Please make sure you are using the latest updated version of our App.
Step 2. please cut off power and reconnect.
Step 3. If some of the iamges aren't showing, it’s normal. This means the network was not strong enough and failed while downloading them.
Please make sure your 2.4G wifi signal is strong and place your doorbell near the router to download.
