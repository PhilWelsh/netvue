Netvue Home-Cam

The multi purpose home-cam is lightweight and easy to install.
Make a video-call from work, keep in touch with friends and relatives, or keep an eye on your children while you're away
Whatever the reason, the Netvue Home-cam is there to stay connected and safeguard your home.

Features

  • *Support android and iPhone smart phone for mobile watching video.No distance limit.
  • *Zero setup for LAN access,APP auto-discover device.
  • *Hassle-free 3steps to setup for W-LAN remote access.
  • *Support up to 32G MicroSD for continuous HD auto-recording.
  • *Remotely playback and download video record.
  • *Remotely assign/share camera access to unlimited relatives/friends without sharing your own account login.
  • *Workable with multiple operation system and browsers on PC.
  • *Send snapshot/on-APP recording to email,social network sites.
  • *Motion detection with notice& alert image to mobile phone.
  • *Night vision,Two-way audio,Digital zoom.
  • *Support WiFi direct configuration without Ethernet cable even for the first time setup, by QRCode scanning.
  • *High security for login management and data encryption.
  • *Auto-reconnection to identifiable network after interference.
  • *Auto-upgrade firmware.
