What does it do?

It's all quite simple really, although it might sound a lot. Our new Wifi Doorbell is able to communicate with your guests directly, we found so many of our users are active or busy a lot of the day and don't have time to answer the door, this is a great reason to buy a wifi doorbell, but what if you're too busy to answer the call, or simlpy don't want to be disturbed? That's where our new product comes in.
It is able to answer the door for you (by your choice), if you are unable to connect or just don't want to deal with smaller troublesome conversations. Your guest will be greeted and can choose to leave a message, a delivery or attempt to contact a specific person. Face recognition will save a person's face and name you know to be added to a list of trusted contacts.

No more missed calls

a missed call is a serious thing, that's why every phone going back to the original mobile and smart phones have had it as a standard feature, you don't know what you will miss, what opportunity, important appointment or conversation you will have missed out on. The wifi doorman is just another step in modern conveniences that are there to add comfort to a busy life
